Game Development Projects

  • Grapple Gunner
    • This is a VR puzzle-platformer that puts the player in a series of steampunk/sci-fi rooms with both obstacle challenges and object-interaction puzzles. The player's goal is to explore each level while achieving grapple-mastery and to ultimately escape the building by beating the final level.
    • Preview version released for Oculus Quest and Oculus Link/Rift
  • Smooch That Dog
    • This is 90's FPS inspired "Kiss em up" where your goal is to cure a group of deranged dogs with the power of love (and some modified firearms that shoot medicine). Explore one floor of the facility and discover 3 unique weapons!
    • Play the game online or download it on Windows or Mac!
